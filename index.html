About Tech2o

TECH2O is an app that avails information on;

Available water sources in a locale - Flactuating underground and surface water levels With the aim of; Mitigating damage caused by calamities e.g flood or droughts. Bad infrastructure planning e.g flooded bridges. Solution for seasonal water bodies SCALING UP With realtime data and more regular data on Kenyan underground and surface water trends Tech2o will.
Avail alerts on when drought/ floods/ calamities are about to hit. Provide platform to report water issues plus suggest solutions to these issues e.g water shortages. Avail alternative water sources during shortages. Locate exact location of nearest water bodies. Avail data on viable economic/social and agricultural activites for particular locale all in real time.
